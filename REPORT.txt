DATA BASE DESIGN

It consist of three related tables: the students, subjects, and grades and these tables are already normalized so that there are no redundant or to avoid redundancy which is repeating like that and to ensure integrity.

ER Diagram:
A student can have many grades and A subject can be taken by many students. The table serve as the relationship between those two(the student and subject).

students (1) -> grades (many)
subjects (1) -> grades (many)
